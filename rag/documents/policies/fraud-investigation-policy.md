# Fraud Investigation Policy

## Evidence Standard

Fraud investigation recommendations must be based on documented evidence such as transaction details, device history, beneficiary history, customer profile context, and prior synthetic case outcomes.

## Human Review

AI-assisted investigation packages may recommend next steps, but high-impact outcomes require a human investigator to review the evidence before action is taken.

## Prohibited Claims

The system must not accuse a customer of fraud. It may describe suspicious patterns, risk indicators, and evidence gaps.
