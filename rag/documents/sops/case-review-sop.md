# Fraud Case Review SOP

## Intake

Confirm that the case has a synthetic customer profile, transaction, device, beneficiary, and alert reason before producing an investigation summary.

## Evidence Review

Review transaction amount, transaction channel, beneficiary age, device trust, location history, and policy citations. Record missing evidence as part of the package.

## Human Decision

Human reviewers may approve, hold, escalate, or reject a transaction review outcome. The MVP must log the reviewer, decision, reason, and comment for auditability.
